About Me

where I am now...

November 2017 I was introduced to cryptocurrency and finally started my coding journey. I had a desire prior to create digitally and need to begin. I started small with a basic HTML/CSS/JS course from codecademy, and have covered fullstack development from React, Node to Mongo. I've made it my mission to become a contributor to the decentralized asset and protocol movement and become an expert and involved developer for the industry.

Where I came from...

I got my start in coding when I was an economics student running statistical and regression analysis through R, SAS, and Shazam Stats Software.

Prior to August 2018 I spent nearly 4 years in corporate retail creating sales plans and doing adhoc analysis for sales and field labor metrics. I experienced a unique blend of technical skills with MS Excel, Access, SQL, ESRI GIS, and being a point person for field managers to communicate the human element in planning and results.
